NYC Open Restaurants

Overview

The focus of my project was the NYC Outdoor Dining program. More specifically, to use several data points from this program and aggreate all dining options around NYC in a simple and efficient way. The OpenRestauarantsNYC project uses open source data from the Outdoor Dining program in addition with pandas, folium, and matplotlib to provide users with meaningful numerical and visual info about restaurants across the city, or in any specified zipcode.

Background and Why?

The Outdoor Dining program was started in NYC to combat COVID spread, it has since become a favorite of many New Yorkers, and a permanent city program. (also show that it is popular and etc.)
